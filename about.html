About Me

Hello! I am a passionate Electronics and Communication Engineering student currently pursuing my degree at Sri Krishna College of Technology. With a strong foundation in both hardware and software, I have developed a deep interest in the intersection of technology and innovation.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained proficiency in programming languages such as C++ and Java, as well as expertise in frontend web development. My experience includes building dynamic web applications, creating user-friendly interfaces, and implementing responsive designs using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I am an enthusiastic learner who constantly seeks out new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set. My projects have ranged from developing a Netflix clone to designing custom 404 error pages and building full-stack web applications using the MERN stack. These experiences have honed my problem-solving abilities and strengthened my understanding of both client-side and server-side technologies.

Beyond my technical skills, I am an effective communicator and a collaborative team player. I enjoy working on group projects and believe that diverse perspectives contribute to innovative solutions. My goal is to leverage my technical expertise and passion for technology to secure exciting core job opportunities in the electronics and communication field.

If you are looking for a dedicated and skilled individual who is eager to contribute to cutting-edge projects and make a meaningful impact, please feel free to connect with me.
